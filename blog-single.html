Jharkhand Income Certificate

Income certificate

  • John Doe
  • Jan 1, 2020
  • 12 Comments

Income certificate is an official document that records the regular income of an individual or a family earned through all sources. Income certificate is used as a legal proof for availing various services and benefits of the Government. The process of issuing an income certificate differs from state to state. In this article, we look at the procedure for obtaining Jharkhand income certificate in detail.

Income certificate is obtained for various purposes, some of which are as follows. The major purpose of obtaining an income certificate is to avail tax relaxations. Students can avail admissions, fee concessions, or apply for any scholarships in educational institutions. The Government facilitates various provisions for the backward classes such as SC/ST for which income certificate is an important certificate to be submitted. Income certificate must be furnished to enjoy the benefits of various welfare schemes implemented by the State and Central government. Agriculture Income Certificate is helpful for getting bank loans as agricultural income is exempted from tax under the Indian Income Tax Act.

The sources of income that are considered for computation are as follows. Income from salary Income of daily labourers Income from business Income of persons employed abroad Income from pension Income from rented buildings Income from property

Documents Required for Income Certificate:

Any person applying for the income certificate in Jharkhand should enclose the following documents along with the application form. Proof of being a native resident of the respective block. The authorities concerned for the issuance of the certificate .

Concerned Authority The authorities concerned for the issuance of the certificate are below. Designated Officer: Block Development Officer / Circle Officer Appellate Authority: First Appeal: Sub Divisional Officer Second Appeal: Deputy Commissioner Fees Self-registered citizens and applicants who apply through Jan Suvidha Kendra can avail the service free of cost. Pragya Kendra (CSC) renders the service . Validity Validity of income certificate would be one year from the date of issuance in Jharkhand. Processing Time The time limit for the processing and issuance of the income certificate is 30 days. Application Procedure The applicant can acquire the income certificate by applying offline or online.
